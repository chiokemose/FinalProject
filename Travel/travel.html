A small collection of the places and people I've met around the world.


Brazil |
Spring 2014



World Cup. Pre-Wedding. Salvador. Natal. Recife. 3 World Cup Matches. Beaches. What more can I say? Oh. Most of the pics are coming soon.

Sleeping Bear Dunes |
August 2015



For our one year anniversary, Maurice and I went camping on the west side of Michigan at Sleeping Bear Dunes. We forgot pillows & rainjackets, but proved our first successful camping trip wasn't just a fluke.

Maine |
May 2015



Camping. This can be a make or break experience for relationships. Maurice and I decided to go to the Acadia National Park to camp _before_ attending a wedding. Why? Because I put camping gear on our registry and we'd literally never camped before.

Arizona |
Fall 2014



Every 5 years, my line sisters get together for an anniversary. (I'm a member of Delta Sigma Theta, Sorority Inc.) We always choose random places that none of us live or would choose to go. Hellooo, Phoenix & Sedona. We had a blast, and now want to go back.

Wedding |
August 2014



A true DIY wedding, we chose Belle Isle, our favorite place in Detroit. It was 3 full days filled with friends, family and a lot of love. Wish we planned better and farther in advance, so we didn't forget to invite folks.

El Salvador |
April 2013



Our first real international trip, El Salvador was the perfect mix of seeing the country up close and personal and relaxation. Photos coming soon.
