ABOUT

I'm a staunch education advocate with a background in the non-profit and for-profit sector. I love working at progressive organizations that make the world a better place, particularly for underresourced and marginalized communities. If I was ever to become independently wealthy, you'd find me advising first-generation college students on the college admissions process (which I currently do on the side) and trying to learn as much as I could about random topics. Oh. And writing, directing and starring in a made-for Syfy movie.

Adaptable

You only need to look at my work history to see that girl can adapt to any role and challenge thrown at her. The most recent example? Coming back from vacation to learn I'd be adding the role of marketer to my job description.

Learner

A good day involves me having a few minutes to research a random topic (like the origins of squirrels) or learning something new for my job ( graphic design anyone?). This website? I built from scratch.

Idea Generator

I create ideas like it's my job. In some awesome roles, it's what I get to do. In others, I just nicely recommend things that come to mind.

Positive

I yearn to be inspired by my work and learn new and better ways to do it. I think because of that, I always remain upbeat and positive, even in the midst of chaos. I always like to see the otherside of a story and see what good can come of mistakes.

Knowledgable

The great thing about constantly learning is that you only continue to grow in your knowledge base. Added to the diversity of my experiences I have a lot of tools in my toolbox.

Performer

You may not think being a performer leads to a better job performance. But it does. Think about it. As a former improv actor, singer and dancer, I'm able to think on my feet in a way that most people think is reserved for Aziz Ansari.

CAREERS

I lead the recruitment and selection process for Grand Circus, a coding bootcamp that train adults to be professional software developers in Detroit. Responsibilities include content & digital marketing, management of CRM system & website, social media, paid ads, and email marketing campaigns.

Director of Community Programs | Grand Circus Detroit

I lead the community program initiatives of Grand Circus, an innovative set of programs that provide training tech opportunities and accelerate careers for the youth, college-aged and adult residents of Detroit. One exciting program we're rolling out in 2016 is a Developer Apprenticeship Program for residents of the Detroit. I encourage you to read, "Why Detroit Needs a Tech Apprenticeship," an article about the program.
